Mathematics deals with the behavior of objects, and not with their essence.

For exemple, a mathematician wouldn't ask what is the number $$1$$ or what is a vector $$v$$. Instead, it would ask about the role $$1$$ plays inside a number system and $$v$$ plays inside a vector space.
